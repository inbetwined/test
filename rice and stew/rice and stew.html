Rice and Stew

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup stew
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water.
  2. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rice and cook on medium heat until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. In a separate pan, heat the stew and add salt to taste.
  5. Serve the rice with the stew on top.

Serving Suggestions:

Serve with a side of vegetables or your favorite protein for a complete meal.
