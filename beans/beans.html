Delicious Beans Recipe

Introduction

This beans recipe is a hearty, nutritious dish that is perfect for a family meal or a cozy dinner. Rich in flavor and easy to prepare, it's a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dried beans (any variety)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Spices such as cumin or chili powder

Instructions

  1. Soak the beans overnight in a large bowl of water.
  2. Drain and rinse the beans, then place them in a large pot with fresh water.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the beans are tender.
  4. In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil and sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until golden.
  5. Add the sautéed onions and garlic to the beans, along with salt, pepper, and any optional spices.
  6. Simmer for another 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

Preparation and Cook Time

Preparation Time: 10 minutes (plus overnight soaking)

Cooking Time: 1.5 - 2 hours
